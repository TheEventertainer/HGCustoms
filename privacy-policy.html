Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At HG Customs, we appreciate the trust you place in us when sharing your personal criteria, custom text, and graphic design assets. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you visit or make a purchase from our store.

1. Information that We Collect

When you make a purchase or attempt to place an order through our site, we collect specific information from you, including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment handling details, email address, and phone number.

Additionally, because we specialize in customizable merchandise, we process and temporarily store custom assets provided by you, such as unique text configurations and uploaded design files, strictly to manufacture your order.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the order information that we collect generally to fulfill any orders placed through the Site (including processing your payment information, arranging for shipping, and providing you with invoices and/or order confirmations). Additionally, we use this info to:

  • Communicate with you regarding design proofs or custom parameters;
  • Screen our orders for potential risk or fraud; and
  • Provide you with information or advertising relating to our custom products where permitted.

3. Asset Retention & Upload Safeguards

Any custom graphic art or images uploaded to our servers are utilized strictly for production validation, formatting, and rendering your tailored goods. These files are securely stored and automatically purged from our operational manufacturing queue within 30 days of completed delivery fulfillment.

4. Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above. For example, we use PayPal to power our secure payment checkout ecosystem. We also use reliable third-party courier services to fulfill physical delivery to your address.
