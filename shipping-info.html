Shipping & Delivery

Last updated: July 2026

Every order from HG Customs is individually made to order. Our delivery timelines are split into two distinct periods: Production Time (crafting your product) and Shipping Time (transit to your address).

1. Production Workflows

Because items are printed, pressed, or assembled individually by hand, please allow 1 to 3 business days for order production before shipment. Peak holiday seasons may introduce mild scheduling variations.

2. UK Delivery Rates & Timelines

We provide tracked, transparent transit calculations across the United Kingdom:

Service Tier Transit Window Cost
Standard Tracked (Orders under £50) 2 - 4 Business Days £3.95
Standard Tracked (Orders £50+) 2 - 4 Business Days FREE
Express Delivery 1 - 2 Business Days £5.95

3. Order Tracking

As soon as your package transfers over to our delivery couriers, a confirmation email containing a tracking link will automatically be generated. You can monitor the path of your package straight to your front door using that information link.

4. Address Accuracy

Please double-check your shipping coordinates during checkout. We cannot assume structural or material liability for items delivered to improperly recorded addresses provided during purchase confirmation.
