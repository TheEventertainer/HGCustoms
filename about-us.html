Our Story

Welcome to HG Customs. We believe that everyday items shouldn't be plain, generic, or mass-produced. Our mission is simple: to help you bring your ideas to life through high-quality, customized craftsmanship.

What started out as a passion project focused on creating custom gifts for friends and family has quickly evolved into a dedicated online storefront. Today, we specialize in high-grade custom printed mugs, tailored t-shirts, and everyday bespoke products designed uniquely by you and brought to life by us.

"Made your way, just for you."
The HG Customs Philosophy

The Craftsmanship Promise

We approach every order individually. Unlike large automation warehouses, we take the time to review your text submissions, align your digital designs correctly, and thoroughly inspect every individual product before it leaves our workspace. We source premium inks, high-grade apparel blends, and sturdy ceramics to ensure your customized memories are built to last.

Why Choose HG Customs?

We are a small, customer-focused operation based right here in the UK. That means when you send us a message, you speak directly with the creators making your items. We offer reliable, fast turnaround speeds, free UK tracking on orders over £50, and a deep commitment to making sure you are completely satisfied with your custom product.
