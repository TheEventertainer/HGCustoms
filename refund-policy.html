Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Because our products are uniquely custom-made to your exact specifications, we operate a specialized return and refund policy to account for personalized craftsmanship.

Custom & Personalized Items

Due to the bespoke nature of our items (mugs, t-shirts, and tailored goods), we cannot accept returns or offer refunds for change of mind or customer errors, including spelling mistakes, layout choice inaccuracies, or incorrect sizing selection.

Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Goods

We take absolute pride in our premium quality standards. If your order arrives damaged during transit, features a structural manufacturing defect, or if we made a mistake that differs from your approved design submission, we will rectify it immediately.

To request a free replacement or full refund for a damaged item:

  • Contact our support team within 14 days of receiving your package.
  • Provide your order number and a clear photo showcasing the item damage or error.
  • Do not ship items back to us without initiating a request, as unauthorized returns cannot be processed.

Cancellations

Orders move into our printing production workflow rapidly. If you need to cancel or modify a custom order, you must notify us within 1 hour of purchase. Once custom materials have been permanently printed or cut, cancellations can no longer be processed.

Refund Issuance

Once an authorized refund is approved, it will be automatically issued back to your original payment method. Please note that banks and credit card issuers can take several business days to post the balance to your account.
